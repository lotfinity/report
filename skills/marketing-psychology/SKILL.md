# marketing-psychology

> Psychological principles for persuasive web experiences.

## What it does

Applies established psychological principles (Cialdini's persuasion, Fogg's behavior model, cognitive biases) to web copy and UI. Covers hook construction, framing effects, anchoring, scarcity, social proof, loss aversion, and pricing presentation. Makes landing pages, reports, and calls-to-action more persuasive without being manipulative.
